Craftvilla

Do you need to destress? Find your inner creative? A day with your spouse away from the kids or a playful afternoon with your kids? Have you always wanted to try painting with proprer watercolors that blend the way you see on instagram? Maybe you’ve painted models with acrylics before and want to try your hands on canvas painting?

Here at Craftvilla, you can do all of the above. We strive to create an open, accepting and welcoming room for you to explore and express yourself. We offer workshops in acrylic painting, watercolors and mosaic composition with rocks and more. Batteries included. For all our workshops. That means we supply all the material you may need, and all you need to do is show up on time and bring your presence. All ages and all groups welcome, from 3 to 103. If you come with particularly young or old family members and need help watching, call ahead and we’ll make sure our staff know to help you out - so that you can focus on defocusing.

Paint on canvas with acrylics

Paint on canvas like the real masters, but in easy mode. Acrylics dry quicker and can be cleaned with water before they dry. No solvants or dangerous chemicals required (just don't eat the paint).

Blend watercolors

on real watercolor paper. Not only are watercolors waterbased (duh), but they blend beautifully as well. With specialiced paper, the paint soaks into the fibers and partially blend inside the paper in interesting ways as well.

Build your own mosaic

using colored stones, rubber and adhesive, compose colorful geometric patterns and shapes. Many find this activity deeply calming and stimulating at the same time. Find your inner Zen or let out your creativity show to the rest of your group.
